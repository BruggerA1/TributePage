Lord Winston

Harvard Business Magnate / Cute Pupper


Lord Winston posing for an oil painting (Circa 2019).

Winston's Greatest Achievements:

  • June 2016: Despite his cool demeanor, Winston is born under the unforgiving blaze of the Arizona sun.
  • August 2016: Adopted by a wealthy family, Winston becomes accustomed to a life of luxury and excess.
  • January 2017: Winston is dealt a poor hand. While being diagnosed with Lymes Disease at the doctor's office, he is attacked by a savage pitbull. Luckily for him, Winston is protected by his father and narrowly escapes death.
  • 2017 - 2019: After flirting with death, Winston finds a new lease on life. Enrolled at the Harvard School of Business, he quickly earns a PhD with a focus on corporate takeovers and LLC agreements.
  • 2020: Winston says goodbye to his home on Strong Island and moves to Worcester, MA in order to become an adjunct professor at his alma mater.
  • 2021 - Present: Winston becomes a highly respected member of his local chamber of commerce. Currently, he is serving as the chapter's President; with his colleague Phineas serving as VP.
"It was a cutthroat business, dog eat dog, the strong preying on the weak. In the lifestyle I was in, people were killed. It happened every day." - Winston
